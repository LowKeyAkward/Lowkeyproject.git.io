Who are we?

As of right now "we" are just a one man show, just trying to run a friendly, fun and informing site for all things cosplay! We review cosplay, props, and conventions based purely on our own opinions. So please try not to get to upset over it.

Whats Cosplay?

So what is cosplay? Cosplay is in my opinion one of the biggest ways of supporting your favorite anime, videogame, comic, movie, tv show etc! Dedicated fans pour blood sweat and tears into their amazing costume and showcase via the internet or convention.
