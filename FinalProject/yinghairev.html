This is the review page for Yinghai_sig's Artoria Pendragon Cosplay, also known as Saber or Arthur Pendragon in the shows lore. The quality of her overall costume is superb, you can tell that the fabric that makes her clothing was not of cheap quality at all, and the craftmanship of her armor is amazing as well. While many people would settle for a low quality silver color, she decided to use a gun metal silver instead, which gives it the shiny sheen you see. She pays very fine attention to her makeup, and hairstyle as well. Overall I would give this cosplay a 9 out of 10 stars, it really blew me out of the water.

If you enjoy her work please follow her instagram at yinghai_sig Thank you!
